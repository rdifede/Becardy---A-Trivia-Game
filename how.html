HOW TO PLAY BECARDY:

  1. Select a category and click on the a value amount to see your question.
  2. Type your answer in the input field, and press submit.
  3. Always answer in the form of a question!
  4. If you answer at least 8 questions correctly, you can move on to Double Becardy!
  5. If you answer at least 8 questions correctly in Double Becardy, you may then proceed to Final Becardy.
  6. Final Becardy is only one question, and you will have 30 seconds to enter a response.

GOOD LUCK!
